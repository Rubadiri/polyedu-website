About Us

Who We Are & What We Do

PolyEdu Health Education Kenya Providers TV is a Kenyan health education platform dedicated to bringing you credible, accessible and practical health knowledge. We are your trusted partner in understanding and managing your health from mental and emotional wellness to physical fitness and preventive care.

Our content is educational in nature and not a substitute for professional medical diagnosis or treatment. We empower Kenyans in all 47 counties with reliable health information to make informed decisions. Whether you're a patient, caregiver or health enthusiast, PolyEdu is here for you every step of the way.

Evidence-based, reliable health education content
Available in English and local Kenyan context
Free for all Kenyans

Our Services

What We Offer You

Comprehensive health education across all dimensions of well-being, designed specifically for families and communities across the world.

Mental Health Education

Understanding anxiety, depression, stress management and building resilience for everyday Kenyans. Break the stigma together.

Emotional Wellness Tips

Practical strategies for emotional regulation, healthy relationships, grief processing, and building positive mental habits daily.

Physical Health Guidance

Exercise tips, body care routines, managing chronic conditions and building sustainable healthy habits suited to Kenyan lifestyles.

Nutrition Advice

Balanced diet guidance using affordable local Kenyan foods, managing diabetes, hypertension nutrition and healthy eating on a budget.

Preventive Care Talks

Cancer screening awareness, vaccination schedules, reproductive health, maternal & child health education across the world.

Live Q&A Sessions

Interactive YouTube live sessions where you can ask health questions directly and get evidence-based answers from our educators.
